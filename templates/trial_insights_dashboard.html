Overview

TrialInsights™ extends Dr2ndOp’s clinician-centered AI assistant with a lightweight patient portal—embedded right within the after-visit summary or accessible via a secure QR code—where patients can explore the clinical trials that underpin their prescribed medications.

Key Capabilities

AI-Curated Trial Summaries

  • Automatically pulls the most relevant Phase II/III trial results (efficacy, safety, endpoints).
  • Plain-language “What You Need to Know” bullets with infographics (responder rates, common adverse events).

Interactive Benefit‑Risk Visualizer

Glossary & Deep‑Dive Links

Tap any medical term for a layperson definition or click below to learn more.

Secure Messaging & Discussion

  • Flag questions to your care team.
  • Asynchronous Q&A between patients, pharmacists, and clinicians.

Integration & Workflow

  • In-Chart Trigger: One-click “Generate Patient Insights” in Dr2ndOp emails the summary link.
  • EHR & Telehealth Hooks: Deep-links into MyChart (Epic) and telehealth chat.

Value Proposition

For Patients

Empowers informed consent—patients see real trial data behind their prescription, boosting confidence and adherence.

For Clinicians & Health Systems

  • Reduces follow-up calls by proactively answering common questions.
  • Supports CMS compliance with “Patient Access to Results” rules.
